The NSVKI is the cooperation for Study Associations for Artificial Intelligence in the Netherlands. The members VIA (Amsterdam), STORM (Amsterdam), Cover (Groningen), CognAC (Nijmegen) and USCKI Incognito (Utrecht) decided it to be a good idea to keep in contact in this form. Our aim is to support Dutch AI students, and foster ties between students in all of the Netherlands.

For information about all the Study Associations see below.

The USCKI Incognito is the study association for all students Cognitive Artificial Intelligence in the departement of philosophy, faculty of humanities at the University of Utrecht.

via, short for 'Vereniging Informatiewetenschappen Amsterdam' (Dutch), is the study association of the UvA for bachelor and master students of the information sciences. This includes the bachelors Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence and Information Science; and the masters: Artificial Intelligence, Computational Science, Computer Science, Information Studies, Logic, Software Engineering and System and Network Engineering. We are also there to support the many minors at the UvA which are associated with our discipline.

STORM is the study association of the departments Mathematics and Informatics from the Faculty of the Exact Sciences at the Free University in Amsterdam. STORM stands for STudent Organisation Mathematicians, but is an association which has long been for more than just mathematics students. Currently, all students who are enrolled in one of the study programs of the aforementioned faculties are able to join the association
